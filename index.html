Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

JobProof ("the App") is designed to help independent professionals document completed work using photo proof and generate PDF reports. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Data Collection

JobProof does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All data created within the App remains on your device unless you choose to share it manually.

2. Camera Usage

The App uses your device camera to capture photos for job documentation. These photos are used to generate before/after reports and are stored locally on your device.

3. Location Data

The App may request access to your location to embed GPS coordinates into photos as proof of work completion. Location data is used only at the moment of photo capture and is not stored or transmitted externally.

4. Local Storage

All information including photos, job details, and generated reports are stored locally on your device. The App does not upload this data to any remote servers.

5. Data Sharing

The App allows you to share reports and photos manually using your device’s sharing features (e.g., email, messaging apps). JobProof does not control how third-party apps handle data after it is shared.

6. Third-Party Services

JobProof does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

JobProof is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
